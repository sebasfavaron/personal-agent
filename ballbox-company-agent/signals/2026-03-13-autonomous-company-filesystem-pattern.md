## What happened

Reference operating model argues for a filesystem-first company OS:

- `.self/` for identity
- `.cycle/` for weekly planning and review
- `signals/` for raw observations
- `worldview/` for beliefs
- `strategy/` for durable convictions
- `projects/` for execution
- `SOP/` for reusable procedures

## Why it might matter

- directly compatible with Ballbox need for agent-readable company state
- reduces drift between company facts, tasks, and operating loops
