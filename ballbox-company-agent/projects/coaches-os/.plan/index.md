# Coaches OS

## Goal

Build a legible Ballbox strategy and discovery loop for the coach workflow wedge.

## Current status

- Ballbox has multiple open fronts, but current concentration is ~99% on coaches.
- This is a soft pivot in focus, not a deletion of the other fronts.
- Current discovery state:
  - no coach form responses synthesized yet
  - no ATC API access wired into Ballbox product yet
  - no customers yet

## Why this exists

- Ballbox now needs a dedicated operating surface for the coach-side product thesis.
- The right job is not to build generic coach software.
- The right job is to find one workflow where Ballbox can win because of ATC-linked context, access, and distribution leverage.

## Current thesis

- user-first focus: student on `/classes`
- coach time-saving is the business rationale
- fallback buyer if needed: club / coordinator
- current leading wedge hypothesis: student-facing class discovery + request
- strongest adjacent wedge: coach/operator coordination workspace
- strategic long-term wedge: reprogramming classes with deeper ATC operational control

## Core constraints

- ATC is strategically indispensable to Ballbox, but not yet integrated in product
- ATC is not formally associated with Ballbox and must not be represented that way
- interviews and form evidence are still sparse
- docs must separate hypotheses from facts
