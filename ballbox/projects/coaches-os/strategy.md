# Coaches OS Strategy

## Role in Ballbox

- This project captures the current highest-focus software wedge for Ballbox.
- It does not replace the machine, screens, or commercial-model fronts.
- It reflects where Ballbox is concentrating discovery and product energy right now.

## Strategic claim

- Ballbox should look for a coach workflow wedge that becomes clearly better because of ATC-connected club/court context.
- ATC leverage is not limited to coaches; it is a Ballbox-wide strategic advantage across multiple fronts.
- Coaches are simply the current highest-focus front for turning that leverage into a product wedge.

## Current operating posture

- discovery mode
- student-first first surface, coach pain relief as rationale
- club/coordinator as acceptable payer fallback
- ATC-core strategy, open on exact integration depth
